01 — Profile

A dual discipline. One lens.

I’m interested in the way accounting and finance help explain how a business is really performing. On the accounting side, I like the structure behind GAAP, reconciliations, retained earnings, and financial statements that actually tie. On the finance side, I enjoy using those numbers to model cash flows, test assumptions, and make stronger business decisions.

At UCF, I’m pursuing a dual B.S. in Accounting & Finance, with plans to continue into a master’s program in Accounting or FinTech. I’m working toward CPA eligibility and am interested in roles across accounting, audit, financial analysis, and business advisory. Outside of class, I’ve helped support finance-related work for Delta Sigma Pi’s 100+ member chapter, lead alumni and corporate partnerships for UCF’s student-run consulting group, and coach students on budgeting, FAFSA, and scholarship strategy.

The work I’m most proud of is the kind that makes things clearer: a three-statement model that balances across projected years, a CRM tracker that replaced a manual process, and a budget that helps people actually understand where the money is going.
